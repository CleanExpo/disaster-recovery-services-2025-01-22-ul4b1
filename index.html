About Us

Founded in 2010, Disaster Recovery QLD began as a family-owned business committed to helping our local community recover from disasters. What started with a single truck and two dedicated employees has grown into the region's most trusted restoration service provider.

Our mission is to provide rapid, professional, and compassionate disaster recovery services while maintaining the highest standards of quality and customer care. We believe in transparency, integrity, and building lasting relationships with our clients through exceptional service.
